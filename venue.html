Conference Venue- Bangalore International Exhibition Center (BIEC)
17th and 18th December 2018

Address:
Bangalore International Exhibition Centre
10th Mile, Tumkur Road, Madavara Post,
Dasanapura Hobli,
Bangalore 562 123
Google Maps Location

How to reach here:

  • By metro: The venue BIEC is just 2 kms away from the last stop, Nagasandra, of the phase two (Green Line) of Bangalore Metro. From there you can either take a public transport or book a taxi.
  • From the airport: Using Uber/Ola/ Airport taxi is the quickest way to get from Bangalore’s Kempegowda International Airport to BIEC. It takes an hour and a half approximately by car to reach the venue. Airport taxis are available immediately after the arrival gate and you have to pay in cash.Uber and Ola zones are also not very far from the arrival gates.
  • By Car: The venue is along the NH48 (Pune Bangalore Highway) and can be easily reached from all parts of Bangalore by public transport as well as taxis.

Workshops Venue - Srishti Institute of Art, Design and Technology
16th December 2018

Address:
Srishti N5 - Studios/Projects/Workshops Campus
CA Site No. 21, 5th Phase, KHB Colony,
Yelahanka New Town,
Bangalore - 560064
Google Maps Location


How to reach here:

  • By metro: The closest metro stations are Jalahalli or Peenya. From here you can get direct buses or cabs to Srishti campus in Yelahanka.
  • From the airport: The Srishti Campus is easily accessible from Bangalore’s Kempegowda International Airport by both the airport shuttles as well as cabs. Using Uber/Ola/ Airport taxi is the quickest way to get from Bangalore’s Kempegowda International Airport to Srishti. It takes 30-45 mins approximately by car to reach the venue. Airport taxis are available immediately after the arrival gate and you have to pay in cash. Uber and Ola zones are also not very far from the arrival gates. The shuttle bus is the most economical option. Bus number KIAS 9 can be taken upto Kogilu Cross and from there a 15 minute auto or cab ride will drop you at the Srishti campus.
  • By Car: The venue is off the NH44 and can be easily reached from all parts of Bangalore by public transport as well as taxis.
